# POS System (收银系统)

A Point of Sale (POS) backend system for service-oriented businesses (spa/massage/wellness centers).

## Core Features
- Order management (开单/结算/冲正/对单)
- VIP membership & recharge system
- Service items & packages management
- Room/bed management
- Stock/inventory management
- KPI & commission tracking
- Multi-tenant organization support

## Business Domain
- Orders: Create, settle, cancel, rollback, reconcile
- VIP: Member registration, balance recharge, ticket/coupon management
- Services: Items, products, packages, cure tickets
- Rooms: Room and bed status management
- Stock: In/out orders, inventory tracking
- System: Users, roles, permissions, organizations, dictionaries
